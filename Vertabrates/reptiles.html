Reptiles

Reptiles are cold-blooded animals with dry, scaly skin.

Most reptiles lay eggs on land.

Examples include snakes, turtles, and crocodiles.

The Komodo Dragon is the biggest lizard in the world.

Cobra can spread its hood when threatened.

Sea Turtle can travel thousands of kilometers in the ocean.

The Chameleon can change color.

Crocodile have very strong bites.

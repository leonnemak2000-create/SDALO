Fish

Fish are animals that live in water.

They breathe through gills and usually have fins and scales.

Examples include goldfish, salmon, and sharks.

The Great White Shark can smell tiny amounts of blood in water

Clownfish live safely among sea anemones.

The Seahorse is a fish where the male carries the babies.

Pufferfish puff up like balloons when scared.

The Swordfish can swim very fast.

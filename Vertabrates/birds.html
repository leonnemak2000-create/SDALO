Birds

Birds are warm-blooded animals covered with feathers.

They have beaks, wings, and most birds lay eggs.

Examples include eagles, parrots, and penguins.

The Ostrich is the biggest bird in the world and cannot fly.

Penguin swims very well but cannot fly.

Parrot can copy human sounds and words.

The Hummingbird can fly backward.
